Overview

A private world of tropical luxury, built for guests and investors alike.

Crystal Valley Estates is a signature-suite resort development on the Mumbai–Goa corridor, positioned directly on NH-66 in Sawantwadi. Developed by O2 Builders and marketed exclusively by Future India Infratech, the project is designed around one idea: a fully-managed holiday residence that works as hard for your rental income as it does for your weekend.

  • Format G+7 resort residences, studio-style 1 BHK fully-loaded service apartments

The Investment Case

Why Sindhudurg, why now.

Sindhudurg is shifting from an agricultural district to a leisure and second-home destination — driven by highway upgrades, two nearby airports and Goa's overflow demand.

%

7.2% regional rental yield

Rental yields across Sindhudurg's holiday-home market run ahead of established hill-station markets like Shimla or Lonavala.

✈

Two airports, one district

MOPA (North Goa) is 30–60 minutes away, and Sindhudurg's own Chipi Airport is moving toward 24x7 operations.

↗

10–15% appreciation expected

Faster Mumbai–Goa connectivity via the coastal highway and NH-66 widening is projected to lift property values across the corridor.

Major upcoming developments nearby

Taj Hotel, Shiroda–Velaghar

A 5-star Taj resort under development, set to lift the broader area's hospitality profile.

Bollywood Film City

A proposed ~₹2,000 crore film city at Nandos village, expected to draw sustained footfall and staff housing demand.

Asia's first underwater museum

Proposed on the Vengurla–Malvan coast, adding to the district's water-sports and tourism draw.

A district built on agriculture, fisheries and tourism

Alphonso mango & cashew

Sindhudurg's Alphonso (Hapus) and cashew are globally known; cashew processing is the district's ODOP product.

121 km of coastline

Malvan, Vengurla and Devgad anchor a fishing economy alongside a fast-growing water-sports and homestay sector.

A strong banking network

SBI, HDFC Bank, Bank of Maharashtra and Union Bank branches serve Kudal, Kankavali and Malvan, with 24x7 ATMs on the highway.
